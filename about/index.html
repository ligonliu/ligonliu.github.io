About

Welcome to my digital corner. By day, I navigate the complexities of data pipelines as a Data Engineer, but this blog serves as a sandbox for my broader technical curiosities.

My writing and research focus on the intersection of the digital and physical realms. I am deeply passionate about simulation, the reasoning of the physical world, and the transformative potential of Design Automation using AI. I explore how we can use AI to not just understand our environment, but to automate the creation of complex systems within it.

Beyond the Code: I am also an advocate for global studies. I volunteer my time to help connect American students with quality higher education opportunities abroad, aiming to broaden horizons and democratize access to knowledge and global roots.
